Welcome

Universal Video Toolkit is installed — one thing to know before it does anything
It's off everywhere by default
  1. Go to any site with video, and click the extension icon in your toolbar.
  2. Toggle "Enabled on this site" — or flip "Enabled everywhere" if you'd rather it just always run.
  3. Hover any video on that site to see the control bar. Drag the pill/bar to reposition it.
Why opt-in? So it only ever does something on sites you've actually chosen, instead of quietly running everywhere. Your site list is remembered — you only do this once per site.
